Welcome to Varentellia

A world under construction — created from scratch, mapped, populated, and illustrated entirely by hand.

A World of Contrast

Varentellia emerged from the same dichotomy that drives the art — the tension between light and shadow, creation and destruction, the ancient and the new.

From the cataclysm-scarred Darklands to the verdant Saga Lands, this world carries the weight of its history in every mountain, forest, and city.

The Peoples of Varentellia

Landwalkers

The most numerous of Men — diverse, ambitious, builders of cities and empires.

Earthfolk

Stewards of stone and soil, connected to the deep places beneath the mountains.

Waterfolk

Dwellers of rivers, lakes, and the deep ocean. Can hold breath for days, dive to impossible depths.

Woodfolk

Scholars of life and magic who mold living plants into dwellings and weapons.

The Saved Ones

Halflings who appeared during the Cataclasm, surrounded by glittering mana.

Grunlings

Once the dominant civilization of Varentellia, now reduced to two hidden cities.

About the Artist

Hi, I'm Drew.

I grew up in Brooklyn, Wisconsin — back when it was 500 people and a bunch of cows. I liked to draw, I loved cartoons, and I spent my summers fishing and building forts in the woods outside of town.

Like a lot of people, I abandoned art for "more respectable" pursuits as I grew up.

Ammunition

I enlisted in the Army in the Summer of 2001. Halfway through basic training, the world changed. What was supposed to be a side quest became the primary mission.

I spent 16 years as an intelligence collector and analyst: Arabic speaker, counter-insurgency, counter-terrorism, special operations. I saw victories and saved lives. I lost friends.

Recovery

Coming back is not easy. Making art is the best means I have found of dealing with the pain. Sometimes it brings peace to feelings of rage. Sometimes it brings hope in feelings of despair.

The more art I sell, the more time I can afford to spend making it. So here I am — making art for anyone who wants it.
