First Year Back

The pieces that marked my return to art

I stopped doing art when I was about 16 — I considered it a vain and childish pursuit. After leaving the defense department, after 16 years fighting the war on terror, two years deployed in Iraq, I needed the healing that art brings. These were my comeback pieces, the primary works for my first year of art shows.

About These Pieces

After 16 years in the fight, I found myself needing something I couldn't get from any other part of life. Art became my therapy, my release, my way of processing what I'd seen and done.

These pieces represent that first year of healing. Some are dark — they come from that place. Some are lighter — those are the glimmers of hope. All of them are me, trying to find my way back to something I abandoned too young.

This is what healing looks like.
