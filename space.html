Beyond the Horizon

Oil. Acrylic. Gouache. Amalgamation.
No AI. All human.

The cosmos has always called to us — that infinite darkness scattered with light, vast and unknowable, simultaneously terrifying and beautiful. These works are my attempt to capture the silence between stars, the weight of loneliness that lingers in the void, and the stubborn hope that drives us to keep looking up.

About This Collection

Space has always fascinated me — the sheer scale of it, the silence, the beauty that doesn't care whether we're watching. These paintings are my love letter to the cosmos, rendered in pigment and pixel.

Each piece explores a different facet of that eternal question: what lies out there, and what does it mean to look up?
