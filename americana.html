About Americana

My connection to public service has shaped how I see America. After years in intelligence work — analyzing, deploying, serving — I found a new way to serve my community. Now working in municipal government, I see firsthand how local ties bind us together.

This series is about the complicated love you can have for a country. The pride and the pain. The symbols that mean so much to some and so little to others. The way we remember, the way we forget, the way we keep going.

From 9/11 to Memorial Day, from the flag to the fireworks — this is my America.
