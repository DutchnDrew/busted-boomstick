About My Miniatures

I've been painting gaming miniatures on and off since I was about 14. What draws me to the hobby isn't the game itself — it's the storytelling and the art. Each figure is a character, a story waiting to be told.

All miniatures are painted with acrylics, carefully layered to bring out detail in armor, robes, weapons, and creature features. No airbrush, no pro shader — just brushes and patience.
